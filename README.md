# The Technical Writer's Edge: A Job Search Guide for the AI Era

> A community-driven guide for technical writers navigating the job market in the age of AI and LLMs.

**Version 1.0 | Last Updated: April 3, 2026**

---

## About This Guide

This guide was distilled from real conversations in the **#career-advice** channel of the [Write the Docs Slack](https://www.writethedocs.org/slack/) — a community of technical writers sharing hard-won experience during a tough job market.

It covers everything from building a job profile that works for both humans and algorithms, to making domain expertise visible, to demonstrating AI fluency in ways hiring managers actually care about.
