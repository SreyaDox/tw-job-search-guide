Key principles from the Write the Docs community

📄 Your Job Profile

  • Rewrite LinkedIn headline with an LLM
  • Keep resume and LinkedIn consistent
  • Build a GitHub Pages portfolio
  • Blog posts get noticed by interviewers
  • Contribute to open source docs

🏗 Domain Expertise

  • Create a dedicated "Domain Expertise" section
  • Name specific technologies, not just areas
  • Describe how you learned each domain
  • Lead with the domain matching each role
  • SME interviewing is a core skill — say so

🤖 AI Fluency

  • Have concrete AI examples ready
  • Prompt engineering = documentation skill
  • Use Claude Code to build your portfolio
  • Take a prompt engineering course
  • Frame AI concerns as things you work within

🎤 Interviews

  • Build a Q&A doc and review it repeatedly
  • Prepare a bank of distinct STAR stories
  • Feed the job desc to an LLM for prep
  • It's fine to admit no direct STAR example
  • Bad interviewers tell you about culture

Rewrite my LinkedIn headline

Rewrite my LinkedIn headline for a senior technical writer role specialising in [DOMAIN]. My background: [PASTE RESUME SUMMARY]. Make it optimised for recruiter searches and immediately clear to a hiring manager. Give me 3 options.

Tailor my resume for a job posting

Here is my resume: [PASTE RESUME]. Here is the job description: [PASTE JOB DESCRIPTION]. Rewrite my resume to closely match the language and priorities of this role. Flag any gaps and suggest how to address them.

Generate STAR interview stories

Based on this resume: [PASTE RESUME], generate 5 strong STAR-format stories (Situation, Task, Action, Result) I could use in a technical writing interview. Focus on: learning complex domains quickly, working with SMEs, handling tight deadlines, and demonstrating AI tool usage.

Copy prompt ↗

Write my Domain Expertise section

Based on my resume: [PASTE RESUME], write a "Domain Expertise" section for my resume and LinkedIn profile. List my domains with specific technologies named. Keep it scannable and optimised for ATS keyword matching. Mirror the language used in this job posting: [PASTE JOB POSTING].

Prepare me for an interview

I have an interview for this role: [PASTE JOB DESCRIPTION]. My background: [PASTE RESUME SUMMARY]. Generate the 10 most likely interview questions, suggest strong answers based on my experience, and help me prepare for questions about AI tool usage. Also flag any likely concerns they may have about my application.

Write a tailored cover letter

Write a concise, confident cover letter for this role: [PASTE JOB DESCRIPTION]. My resume: [PASTE RESUME]. Lead with my most relevant domain expertise. Include a specific example of how I've used AI tools in my work. Keep it under 300 words and avoid clichés.
